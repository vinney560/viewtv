Vision & Philosophy

View TV is not just a platform — it's a vision to provide a seamless, lightweight, and intuitive viewing experience. I created this platform to give users access to categorized live streams, both local and global, with secure access control.

Channel Sources

We use publicly accessible, legal streaming URLs. Examples include:

  • Free-to-air Kenyan and regional stations
  • Manually added streams from M3U8/HLS URLs
  • Admin-controlled custom_list entries
  • Plus-protected streams via secure token access

View TV does not host or rebroadcast any copyrighted content. Our goal is to aggregate safe, viewable, categorized public content.

Technologies Used

  • Backend: Flask (Python), Jinja2, JWT, WTForms, SQLite
  • Frontend: TailwindCSS, responsive UI, SVG icons, glow effects
  • Authentication: Flask-Login, password hashing, role-based access
  • Security: CSRF protection, rate limiting, session control
  • Deployment: Hosted on Render, SEO with sitemap.xml and robots.txt
